## Description

The tool shall accept externally generated requirement suggestions.

## Rationale

AI-assisted requirement generation enables reverse engineering and accelerates development.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Imported items are marked as Draft
- [ ] Existing requirements are not overwritten
- [ ] Validation of IDs and hierarchy is enforced
