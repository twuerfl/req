## Description

The tool shall be structured into modules with clearly defined qualification boundaries.

## Rationale

Separation minimizes certification scope and ensures safe isolation of critical components.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Core, engine, CLI, and MCP modules are separated
- [ ] Dependency direction is strictly enforced
- [ ] Qualified modules contain no unsafe operations
