## Description

The tool shall read, manage, and version High Level Requirements (HLR), Low Level Requirements (LLR) and Test Requirements (TST) from markup files (Markdown/YAML).

## Rationale

Centralized, structured management of HLRs is necessary to ensure consistency, traceability, and long-term maintainability. Without centralized management, HLRs could become redundant, incomplete, or inconsistent, jeopardizing the foundation for LLR and traceability.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] HLR/LLR are automatically assigned unique IDs
- [ ] IDs are immutable and never reused (Safety Critical)
- [ ] Parent-child links (HLR→LLR→TST) must be auditable
- [ ] Changes are versioned (via Git)
- [ ] Draft/Approved status is supported
