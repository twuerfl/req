## Description

The tool shall detect superficial or invalid implementations that falsely satisfy requirement coverage.

## Rationale

AI-generated artifacts can artificially satisfy metrics without real functionality, compromising safety and quality.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Detection of trivial implementations
- [ ] Test validity checks
- [ ] Mapping of coverage quality to requirements
