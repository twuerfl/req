## Description

The tool shall integrate with CI systems to validate traceability.

## Rationale

Continuous validation prevents degradation of requirement quality.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] CI failure on broken traceability (strict mode)
- [ ] Optional non-blocking reporting mode
- [ ] Deterministic results
